%Tip 1
>You can use the Home key to toggle graphics mode on and off - useful if Ebonhack graphics are covering text.

%Tip 2
>If your graphics look messed up, select Options from the Game menu and disable DEC graphics mode.

%Tip 3
>You can use the arrow keys to move your character.

%Tip 4
>Shift-click on a monster or dungeon feature to display its name.

%Tip 5
>You can chat with your fellow players on IRC. Server: irc.freenode.net Channel: #nethack

%Tip 6
>You can get more accurate graphics by adding OPTIONS=vt_tiledata to your nethack options file.
